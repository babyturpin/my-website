Top Apps That Pay You in Kenya 💰

In this post, we’ll look at some real apps you can use to make money using your phone in Kenya.

1. Survey Apps

These apps pay you to answer simple questions. You earn small amounts, but it adds up over time.

2. Freelancing Apps

If you have skills like writing, design, or typing, you can get paid jobs online.

3. Task-Based Apps

These apps pay you to complete simple tasks like watching videos or testing apps.

Tips to Succeed

  • Use multiple apps
